After years of the old animated-background landing page (still alive over in the
[archive](/archive)), I finally gave this place a refresh. The plan is simple:

- **A spot to write.** You're reading the first post. New entries are just
  Markdown files dropped into the repo — see the next post for how that works.
- **The birds.** I spend most of my free time photographing the birds around
  North Andover. The best of them now live at
  [birds.scott-ouellette.com](https://birds.scott-ouellette.com), pulled straight
  from [@birdsofnorthandover](https://www.instagram.com/birdsofnorthandover/) and
  ranked by likes.
- **Everything else.** Other [photography](/photography) gets its own corner too.

The old experiments aren't gone — the collision, tilt-shift, and voronoi
backgrounds still rotate behind this very page. More soon.
